# Act 4: Agentic Research

## Context

Final stage of the CASBO 2026 demo. This is the showpiece — fully agentic
AI conducting deep research to uncover hidden patterns that no simple query
would find.

## Data

- `data/survey_full_dataset.json` — pre-joined survey + HR data (500 records, 20 fields)

## Goal

Demonstrate agentic AI autonomously:
- Formulating hypotheses
- Running subgroup analyses
- Discovering hidden patterns (Hillcrest wing anomaly, transfer paradox,
  experienced newcomer effect, Valley High bright spot)
- Writing a research memo with findings, evidence, and caveats

## What to Show

- The "wow factor" of AI discovering non-obvious insights
- How agentic loops work (hypothesis → test → refine)
- Responsible interpretation — correlation vs. causation, sample sizes, caveats
- This is almost entirely live Claude Code. The webapp serves as a dashboard
  showing the research log and emerging findings.
